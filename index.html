About Me

My Journey

I started my programming journey at age 13 when I decided to learn game development out of curiosity and passion. Playing my favorite games made me wonder how they were designed and built, and I wanted to understand the creative and technical process behind them.

Currently, I'm diving deep into Pygame for 2D game development, creating everything from arcade classics to original puzzle games. I'm also building practical game applications using Tkinter to solve real-world problems.

My next future goal is to add Web development and Data science to my field of knowledge—learning html, css, and javascript to build websites and then, pandas, matplotlib, and numpy to uncover insights from data and create compelling visualizations.
